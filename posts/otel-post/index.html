Great news! FindOptimal has integrated another global OTA - Otel.com. From now on, our hotel meta-search will include more than 220K hotels globally offered by otel.com. If a hotel deal from otel.com is attractive to you, you can click on the otel.com icon and jump to their booking site directly. Otel.com is an online travel agency operated by MegGlobal Group. MetGlobal group also owns a hotel wholesale business - HotelPro. As its B2C solution, Otel.com offers prices different from mainstream OTAs such as Expedia and Booking.com. Therefore, you will have a chance to get a better price on the same hotel room from Otel.com. More information about Otel.com is available at here.
