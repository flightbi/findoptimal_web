To provide better room rates for you, FindOptimal integrated the direct offerings from Choice Hotels International’s website. In most cases, the price from Choice is a couple of dollars cheaper than those offered from Online Travel Agencies. Additionally, you may enjoy all benefits of direct booking, such as Choice Privileges reward. No additional action is required from the user side. You search hotels as usual. If a Choice hotel is founded, the result will have a link named Hotel Website to the Choice booking page with Choice’s price displayed behind the link. Choice Hotels International, Inc. is a hospitality holding corporation based in Rockville, Maryland, in the United States. The company owns the hotel and motel brands Comfort Inn, Comfort Suites, Quality Inn, Sleep Inn, Clarion, Cambria Hotel & Suites, Mainstay Suites, Suburban Extended Stay, Econo Lodge, Rodeway Inn, and Ascend Hotel Collection. It manages 6,379 properties and 505,278 rooms, primarily in North America. The average room price is approximately $45.80 per night.
