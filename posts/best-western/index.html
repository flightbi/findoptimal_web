Good news! FindOptimal has integrated direct hotel booking with Best Western. You may enjoy all benefits of direct booking, such as Best Western reward. No additional action is required from your side. You search hotels as usual. If a Best Western hotel is founded, the result will have a link named Hotel Website to the Best Western booking page with direct booking price displayed behind the link. Best Western International, Inc. operates over 4,100 hotels and motels worldwide, about half of which are in North America. It headquarters in Phoenix, Arizona, United States. The company organizes its hotel and motel brands in a three-tiered system: Best Western, Best Western Plus, and Best Western Premier.
