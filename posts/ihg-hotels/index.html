It is our pleasure to announce the integration with InterContinental Hotels Group PLC (IHG)’s hotel offering. No additional action is required from the user side. You search hotels as usual. If an IHG hotel is founded, the result will have a link named Hotel Website to the IHG booking page with IHG’s price displayed behind the link. InterContinental Hotels Group PLC (IHG) is a multinational hotels company headquartered in Denham, UK. IHG has over 5,028 hotels and 742,000 rooms across nearly 100 countries. Its brands include Candlewood Suites, Crowne Plaza, EVEN Hotels, Holiday Inn, Holiday Inn Express, Hotel Indigo, Hualuxe, InterContinental, Kimpton Hotels and Resorts and Staybridge Suites.
