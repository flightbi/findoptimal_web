We have integrated 141,462 properties provided by the Only-apartments platform. From now on, you can find more alternative accommodation choices (short rental apartments) besides hotels from our search site, especially in Europe. Only-apartments specializes in apartment rentals for short and medium stays. The company aims to offer the widest selection of apartments at a lower price than hotels. It is based in Barcelona, Spain and currently lists over 140,000 apartments in 109 countries worldwide. The company's shares have been traded on Alternative Spanish Stock Exchange (MAB) since 2014.
