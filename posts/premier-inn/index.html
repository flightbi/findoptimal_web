Good news for travelers looking for budget hotels in the United Kingdom. We have completed the integration with Premier Inn. From now on, Premier Inn will be included in the result when you search budget accommodations in the United Kingdom or Ireland. Premier Inn is the UK's largest hotel brand, with more than 65,000 rooms and 750 hotels. It operates hotels in a variety of locations including city centers, suburbs and airports. Premier Inn focuses on direct sales. So you will miss this great option if you search hotels with Online Travel Agencies.
