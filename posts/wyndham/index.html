With direct booking, you may get a hotel room at good price and enjoy the benefit of Wyndham Rewards. FindOptimal labels the direct links as Hotel Web in the search result.

Introduction to Wyndham Hotel

Wyndham Hotel Group is the world’s largest and most diverse hotel business. It has a global portfolio of more than 8,100 hotels and over 705,700 rooms in 79 countries. It manages iconic brands including Dolce, Wyndham Grand, Wyndham, Wyndham Garden, TRYP, Wingate, Hawthorn, Microtel, Ramada, Baymont, Days Inn, Super 8, Howard Johnson, Travelodge US and Knights Inn. All those brands are united by the Wyndham Rewards. The program offers members a simple-to-use, generous points earning structure along with a flat, free night redemption rate – the first of its kind for a major rewards program in the business.
