For example, when you register with FindOptimal, we ask for your name and email address. If you login with Facebook or Google, you are permitting us to use and store that information related to the account. When you give us personal information, you are telling us that the information is true, accurate, complete and current. You are also telling us that you have the authorization to provide it to us. FindOptimal may combine information about you that we have with information we obtain from suppliers, business partners, or other companies. In addition, FindOptimal automatically receives and records information in our server logs from your browser, including your IP address, browser type, and the pages you request. This information does not necessarily enable us to personally identify you. Information Use FindOptimal may use your personal information for the following purposes: To verify your authorization to access certain features of the site, to fulfill your requests for products or services, to personalize and customize the content and relevant trip services that you are offered; to contact you about new products, services, special events and promotions, unless you have opted out; to conduct contests, sweepstakes and promotions, if you signed up to participate; to help us learn about your travel preferences and to enhance your booking experience; to help us improve our products and services; and to provide reporting. Information Sharing and Disclosure FindOptimal does not rent, sell, or share personal information about you with other people or companies except (i) to provide products or services you have requested including, without limitation, products and services from third parties, (ii) when we have your permission, or (iii) under the following circumstances: FindOptimal may use third party vendors to assist us in providing our products or services and may transfer your personally identifiable information to such vendors who have entered into a contract with us that protects the confidentiality of your information and permits the vendor to use it only as necessary to fulfill its contract; In response to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process, or to establish or exercise our legal rights or defend against legal claims; When we believe it is necessary to share information in order to investigate, prevent, or take action regarding illegal activities, suspected fraud, situations involving potential threats to the physical safety of any person, violations of FindOptimal 's Terms of Service, or as otherwise required by law; We may transfer information about you if FindOptimal is acquired by or merged with another company. In this event, FindOptimal will notify you by sending a notice to the email address specified in your FindOptimal account before information about you becomes subject to a different privacy policy. From time to time, we also may share general information about our Site, users, and visitors such as the number of visitors, aggregate and/or non-personally identifiable demographic and travel data, etc., with third parties. Cookies FindOptimal sets and accesses cookies on your computer. A cookie is a small amount of data that FindOptimal's servers transfer to your browser and that only FindOptimal's servers can read. When you visit the site, the cookie captures specific session data to streamline your experience and, if you select to save your user account and password, FindOptimal will save this information in a cookie so that you will not be required to sign in each time you visit the site. You may disable or delete cookies from within your Internet browser application by accessing the security or privacy settings. FindOptimal may allow other companies that show advertisements on some of our pages to set and access their cookies on your computer. Other companies' use of their cookies is subject to their privacy policies, not this one. Other companies do not have access to FindOptimal's cookies. For modern browsers, FindOptimal uses local storage technology to store your account and password, if the "Save password" box is checked on the Login window. Local storage is a persistent type of storage, that provides similar functionality to cookies, but its contents are not transmitted across the Internet. Like cookies, you can control how local storage works on your browser settings on a global or website specific manner. We also use Google Analytics to help us understand how users use our web site and our mobile applications. Google Analytics cookies do not provide FindOptimal with any personally identifiable information. They can tell us things like how you arrived at the FindOptimal site, if you have visited the site before, how long you stay on the site, and which pages on the site you visited. They can also provide us with general information about where in the world a user may be located. This information helps us understand how people use our site and our products, and it gives us information about the kind of experience people have on our site and with our products, all of which helps us learn how to make theFindOptimal experience even better.
